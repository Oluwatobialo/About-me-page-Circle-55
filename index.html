About Me

Hi there! My name is Oluwatobi Alo, and I'm thrilled to have you visit my "About Me" page. I was born on July 24th, which makes me a proud Cancer. Although I hail from Osun state, I currently reside in Lagos, Nigeria.

I'm a student of Applied Physics and Electronics at the University of Lagos, and I'm passionate about learning and always eager to expand my knowledge in various fields. In addition to my studies, I also have some work experience. Recently, I interned at BuffaloGrid, a UK-based company that uses a blend of solar-powered hardware and smart software to bring offline streaming and content to disconnected communities. It was an incredible experience, and I learned a lot during my time there..

I'm also passionate about my hobbies, which include playing competitive basketball, swimming, drawing, and painting. These activities help me to relax, unwind, and keep me grounded.

I'm excited to be a part of the AltSchool community. I first heard about AltSchool on Twitter, and seeing my friend go through the program and grow convinced me to join as well. So far, my journey at AltSchool has been amazing, and I'm learning a lot from the system, which gives me the freedom to engage in other activities.

My ultimate goal is to learn the basics so that I can build on that. I'm surrounded by great people in my AltSchool circle, and I hope to get to know them well enough to consider them friends by the end of the program.

Thank you for taking the time to read my "About Me" page, and I hope to get to know you better as well.
